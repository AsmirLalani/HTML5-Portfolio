The Best Animated Movies According to Asmir

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Cars

Racing, redemption, and a heartwarming story of friendship. This movie is a cinematic masterpiece and anyone that disagrees probably probably liked watching National Geographic as a kid.

Finding Nemo

As much as I don't support parents that can't keep track of their kids, Marvin abandoning his child led to one of the most iconic animated movies of all time. Nemo single-handedly brought back the demand for clownfish at every pet store across America.

Inside Out

If you thought you couldn't get emotional watching an animated movie, you haven't seen Inside Out. Bing Bong's sacrifice still brings a tear to my eye. Top 3 movie deaths of all time and it's not even close.
